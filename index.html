PTG Forum

PTG stands for Price Transportation Group. PTG was initiated by a group of PhD students and researchers to enable active collaborations and knowledge exchange among researchers who study transportation at the USC Price School. The main goal of the PTG is to offer an "informal" academic forum to allow exchange of knowledge, ideas, and experience among a group of doctoral researchers pursuing various transportation research topics.
