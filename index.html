PTG Forum Homepage

PTG stands for Price Transportation Group. PTG was initiated by a groupd of PhD students and researchers to enable active collaborations among researchers and scholars who study transportation at the USC Price School. The main goal of the PTG is to offer an "informal" academic forum to allow exchange of knowledge, ideas, and experience among a group of doctoral researchers pursuing various transportation research topics.
